Exclusive Invite to RSA Fraud & Risk Intelligence Solution Week
We would like to invite you to a closed-door insights sharing with RSA’s Fraud & Risk Intelligence Team, visiting from the RSA Anti-Fraud Command Centre from 26th – 29th Nov, 2018.

The impact of digital transformation against business priorities has never been more challenging with the evolution of the cybercrime landscape. Cybercrime continues to plague organizations as demonstrated by 2018 numbers. A phishing attack is launched every 30 seconds and accounts for 48% of all cyberattacks. Over 4 billion account credentials have been compromised causing account takeover fraud losses to triple. Mobile is the fastest growing channel for fraud and represents over 70% total fraud losses.

Hear first-hand on how RSA gives organizations who are transforming their digital, multi-channel strategy the ability to protect consumers against fraud while improving the user experience and reducing transaction friction.

You will learn about:
• Card-not-present fraud and the impact of 3D-Secure 2.0
• The latest trends in phishing, malware, social media threats and other cyber attacks
• Risk management strategies for digital banking transformation
